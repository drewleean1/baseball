The Sandlot League

Your one stop shop for everything Sandlot League related:

  • Add, delete, or edit a team
  • Add, delete, or edit a player
  • Views trades and the players/teams involved in those trades

About our Site

This is a simple baseball themed database that allows users to keep track of the Teams and Players for any local league. Each team entry will consist of a team name, mascot, and a home playing field. Players information will include: name, age, the team they play for, and four stat categories. These stat categories will be a ranking of a player's skill from 1-10 based upon the given skill criteria (Batting, Running, Fielding, and Pitching). A user can view each player's position by clicking on the so named tab on the navigation bar. If a player has multiple positions, they will be listed for every position that they play.

To prevent any confusion or duplicate names, we have added error checking for any new team or player added to the database. No new player or team can be added to the database if there is already a player/team with that name.

Players can be traded to different teams for other players, and we have a section of the site where trades can be added. A trade can be added by submitting a form that includes the date, the players, and the teams involved. Additionally, there is a section that lists the players/teams from all the trades in table form for easier consumption.

If for some reason a team gets a new home field, or a player decides to change their name, both teams and players have edit buttons allowing those changes to be made. If a player moves to a new city and is no longer in the league, a delete button is also included to remove a player from the database. The same is true for for teams.
